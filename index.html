    Designed pixel-by-pixel to look great at small sizes but scale up elegantly.

    We designed Maki with the goal of creating an international, comprehensive, and stylistically unified point of interest icon set. In order to maximize crispness and clarity, we've made three size variations of each symbol. Use all three sizes to scale the icons with the map as you zoom in and out.

    • 24px
    • 18px
    • 12px

    Universal

    Before beginning each icon, we look for a common symbol that has recognition across cultures.

    Made for Mapping

    Size and simplicity are the top design priorities for Maki, so the icons will look clear but won't crowd your map.

    Legible

    All the icons include a subtle border so they stand out against complex or dark backgrounds.

    Open Source

    Visit the GitHub repository to fork, contribute, and provide feedback on Maki.
